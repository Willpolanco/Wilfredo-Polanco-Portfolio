about us

History about Pizzer.io

Pizzer.io was born from a simple yet powerful idea: combining two passions that unite people around the world: video games and pizza. We realized that many gamers often play at home or online, but there was a lack of physical spaces where they could gather, share, and enjoy their passion as a community.

That's when the question arose: why not create a place where video games and food merge into a unique experience? Thus was born Pizzer.io, a pizzeria with a game room designed for all types of gamers. Here you can not only enjoy a delicious pizza, but you can also compete in tournaments, play with friends, and relive the nostalgia of classics on our arcade machine.

Pizzer.io isn't just a restaurant; it's a meeting place for gamers, a space where food and fun go hand in hand. Our goal is to offer not only the best flavor, but also the best experience, with an inclusive and energetic atmosphere.

Whether you're a casual player or a serious competitor, Pizzer.io always has a controller waiting for you and a freshly baked pizza ready to accompany your game.

Our Vision

Mission, Vision, and Values

Vision

To be the leading arcade pizzeria and benchmark in the industry, recognized for the perfect fusion of flavor, technology, and entertainment. We seek to expand our experience regionally, creating spaces where creativity, innovation, and a passion for gaming integrate with culinary excellence.

Values

Quality: We use fresh, select ingredients to ensure that each pizza is a delight for the palate. Innovation: We reinvent the traditional pizza eating experience. Fun: We believe that food and entertainment are the key to enjoying every moment; that's why we strive to create environments full of energy and creativity. Community: We foster an inclusive space where all pizza and video game lovers can connect, share, and grow together. Commitment:We strive every day to exceed our customers' expectations, guaranteeing exceptional service and unforgettable experiences.

Mission

We are dedicated to offering a unique experience that combines a passion for pizza and arcade entertainment. We are committed to serving high-quality pizzas in a fun, innovative, and welcoming environment, where every customer feels part of a gaming community and enjoys memorable moments.
